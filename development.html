FHG Builders — Development

We build where we
believe.

FHG Development pursues ground-up projects and joint ventures in the Denver metro where disciplined execution creates durable value. We are builders first — which means we underwrite a deal knowing exactly what it costs to deliver, because we're the ones delivering it. We don't chase volume or speculative upside. We partner on projects we'd be proud to put our own name on, and we bring the same enterprise-grade management to a development pro forma that we bring to a job site.

01

Builders who develop

We come to development from the construction side, not the other way around. That means our budgets reflect real cost, our schedules reflect real sequencing, and our risk is priced by people who've actually built. Capital partners get a developer who can't fool themselves about what a project takes.

02

Aligned, not extractive

We structure ventures so our interests sit alongside our partners', not against them. We make money when the project succeeds — through delivering a quality asset on budget, not through fees layered on a deal that doesn't pencil. We'd rather do fewer projects well.

03

Long-term value

We build assets meant to perform for years, not to be flipped on a spreadsheet. Quality of construction, soundness of location, and integrity of the underwriting all matter. When we put our name on a development, we're underwriting our reputation alongside the capital.
